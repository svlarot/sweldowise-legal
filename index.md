# Privacy Policy for Sweldo Wise

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

This policy explains what information Sweldo Wise collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've written it in plain language because we believe you should be able to understand it without a lawyer.

If anything here is unclear, email us at **support@sweldowiseph.com** and we'll explain.

## Who can use Sweldo Wise

Sweldo Wise is intended for users **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a person under 18 has used the app, please contact us at **support@sweldowiseph.com** and we will delete their information.

---

## What information we collect

### Information you give us directly

When you sign up:
- Your **name**
- Your **email address**

When you use the app, you may also enter:
- **Financial information** — your salary, expenses, savings goals, debts, and other money-related details
- **Personal preferences** — settings, categories, goal names, and similar choices you make

You enter all financial information manually. **Sweldo Wise does not connect to your bank accounts, GCash, credit cards, or any payment service.** We never see your bank balance, account numbers, or transactions unless you type them in yourself.

### Information collected automatically

When you use the app, we automatically collect:
- A unique account identifier (so we can save your data to your account)
- Basic technical information needed for the app to work (such as login session tokens)

We **do not** use analytics tools, behavioral tracking, or crash reporting services that identify you personally.

### Information from advertising (free version only)

The free version of Sweldo Wise shows ads through **Google AdMob**. We use **non-personalized ads only**, which means ads are not targeted based on your behavior or interests.

For non-personalized ads, AdMob may still collect:
- Your device's advertising identifier
- Approximate location (city level, derived from IP address)
- Basic device information (model, operating system version)
- Information needed to prevent fraud and limit how often you see the same ad

This information is collected directly by Google, not by us. Google's handling of this data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

The paid version of Sweldo Wise does not show ads and does not share data with AdMob.

## How we use your information

We use your information only to:

1. **Provide the app** — let you log in, save your data, and sync it across your devices
2. **Show you the right ads** in the free version (non-personalized only)
3. **Respond to your support requests** when you email us
4. **Comply with the law** if we receive a valid legal request

We **do not**:
- Sell your information to anyone, ever
- Share your financial data with advertisers
- Use your information to train AI models
- Send you marketing emails (we don't send any emails except in response to your support requests)

---

## Where your information is stored

Your account information and the data you enter are stored on **Supabase**, a cloud database service. Our Supabase project is hosted in Northeast Asia (Seoul, South Korea — ap-northeast-2).

If you live outside that region — for example, if you are an OFW in Europe, the Middle East, or North America — your data will travel from your device to that server. We rely on Supabase's standard security measures (encryption in transit and at rest) to keep your data safe during this transfer.

For users in the European Union and United Kingdom: South Korea has been recognized by the European Commission as providing an adequate level of data protection (Adequacy Decision of December 2021), so your data can be transferred there under standard GDPR rules. Supabase publishes its compliance documentation at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

---

## How long we keep your information

We keep your information for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account (see below), we will delete your personal information from our active database within **30 days**. Some information may remain in encrypted backups for up to **90 days** before those backups are rotated and overwritten.

We may keep some non-personal information in anonymized form (such as aggregate counts of users) for analytical purposes, but this cannot be linked back to you.

## Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the information we hold about you
- **Correct** information that is wrong
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** a copy of your data in a readable format
- **Withdraw consent** for any data processing at any time
- **Complain** to a data protection authority (see below)

To exercise any of these rights, email **support@sweldowiseph.com**. We will respond within **30 days**.

### How to delete your account

You can request account deletion in two ways:

1. **In the app** — go to Settings → Delete Account, and follow the steps. *(Note: this feature is being added; if it isn't available yet in your version of the app, please use option 2.)*
2. **By email** — send a deletion request to **support@sweldowiseph.com** from the email address you used to sign up. We will verify the request and delete your account within 30 days.

Deletion is permanent. We cannot recover your data after it has been deleted.

## Security

We protect your information using:

- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS) when your data travels between your device and our servers
- **Encryption at rest** on Supabase's database servers
- **Password hashing** so we never store your password in readable form
- **Limited access** — only the developer can access the database, and only when necessary

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever discover a security incident that affects your information, we will notify you by email within **72 hours** of becoming aware of it, as required by Philippine and EU law.

## Cookies and similar technologies

Sweldo Wise is a mobile app and does not use cookies in the traditional web-browser sense. The app does store small files on your device for things like keeping you logged in and remembering your preferences. These are not used for tracking.
